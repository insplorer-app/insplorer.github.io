Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Insplorer respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not have servers that collect or store your personal information. Session data is stored securely in the device's encrypted Keychain.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Your data never leaves your device.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by logging out or uninstalling the app. Use "Clear Cache" in Settings to remove cached analytics data.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures including iOS Keychain encryption for sensitive data.

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
